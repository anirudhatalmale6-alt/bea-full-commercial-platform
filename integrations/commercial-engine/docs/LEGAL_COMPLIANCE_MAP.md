# Legal and Compliance Page Map

This code pack includes routes and starter records for the following pages:

- Terms and Conditions
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy
- Refund Policy
- Safeguarding Policy
- Parent Consent Policy
- Child Data Protection Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Accessibility Statement
- AI Content Disclosure
- Complaints Policy
- Teacher / Tutor Terms

Production rule: these starter pages must be reviewed by a qualified legal/compliance professional before launch.

Child data rule: parent-child linking, parental consent, access control and report visibility must be enforced before any child learner progress data is displayed.
