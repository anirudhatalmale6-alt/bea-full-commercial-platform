# BEA Pricing Strategy

## Pricing position

BEA should launch as an affordable, self-paced English LMS with a low-friction paid Level Test and accessible course prices.

The strongest commercial sequence is:

```text
Public preview -> Paid Level Test -> Mapped pathway -> Short trial lesson -> Full course payment
```

## Why this structure works

- The Level Test creates learner commitment before course purchase.
- The mapped pathway makes the course recommendation feel personalised.
- The short trial lesson gives learners confidence before buying the full course.
- £27 launch course pricing keeps entry accessible.
- Bundles and subscriptions increase lifetime value.
- Institution licences create higher-value B2B revenue.

## Public pricing language

Use:

- "Level Test, Score Report and Diagnostic Certificate"
- "Short trial lesson unlocked after your Level Test"
- "Full course access after payment"
- "Launch price"
- "Institution licences available"

Avoid:

- "Official CEFR certificate"
- "Cambridge certificate"
- "Guaranteed level"
- "University accepted certificate"

## Pricing governance

- Platform admin can hide/show plans.
- Platform admin can update display price text.
- Stripe Price IDs must be created and updated safely.
- Historical payments must keep the price paid at time of purchase.
- Coupons must have limits, expiry and audit logs.
