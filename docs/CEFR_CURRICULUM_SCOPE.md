# CEFR Curriculum Scope

The live seed curriculum contains 36 modules: six modules at each CEFR level from A1 to C2. Each module includes outcomes, vocabulary fields, grammar focus, lesson sequence, assessment evidence and differentiation support.

## Course structure

- A1: Foundation communication and survival English
- A2: Everyday tasks, travel, health and short narratives
- B1: Independent communication, opinions and familiar work/study contexts
- B2: Argument, reports, academic reading and professional communication
- C1: Advanced evidence, nuance, policy, presentations and professional writing
- C2: Mastery discourse, rhetoric, synthesis and high-stakes communication

## Content expansion plan

The seed curriculum is ready for platform deployment. For commercial launch at scale, the content team should add:

- 12-20 full lesson pages per module
- teacher guides and downloadable worksheets
- listening audio scripts and recordings
- speaking rubrics with examiner calibration samples
- writing rubrics with anchor scripts
- item-review metadata and item retirement workflow
