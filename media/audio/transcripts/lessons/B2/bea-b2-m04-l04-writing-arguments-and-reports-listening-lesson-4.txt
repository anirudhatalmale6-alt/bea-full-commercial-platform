Explanation.
In this lesson you will practise listening for writing arguments and reports. You will learn useful language connected to academic topics and practise advanced passives. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.
Model examples.
Example 1: I can use advanced passives to talk about academic topics.
Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.

Question.

Option A. A clear answer using the target language.
Option B. An answer with missing information.

Speaking practice.
