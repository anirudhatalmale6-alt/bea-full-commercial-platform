Listen carefully. You will hear the model once, then answer the question.
Learning objective: Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a listening task about technology.
Today you will practise language connected to technology.
Target language: reported speech basics.
Explanation.
In this lesson you will practise listening for independent communication and opinions. You will learn useful language connected to technology and practise reported speech basics. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.
Model examples.
Example 1: I can use reported speech basics to talk about technology.
Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
Now listen to the short task.
Speaker one: I am practising technology.
Speaker two: I need to use reported speech basics correctly.
Speaker one: Listen again and choose the answer that best matches the meaning.
Question.
Which answer best shows the lesson objective for B1 level?
Option A. A clear answer using the target language.
Option B. An answer with missing information.
Option C. An answer that does not match the situation.
Correct answer: Option A.
Speaking practice.
Speak for 60-120 seconds about technology. Use at least two examples and one reason.
Writing practice.
Write a short B1 response about technology. Include the target language: reported speech basics.
