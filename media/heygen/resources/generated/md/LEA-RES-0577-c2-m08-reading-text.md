## Reading text
In this lesson, learners explore stories, literature and creative expression. They listen, speak, read and write with a clear purpose. The teacher models the language, then learners practise together. Mistakes help the teacher choose the next step. When learners are ready, they add more detail, better vocabulary and clearer organisation.

## Questions
- Find the main idea.
- Find two details.
- Which words are connected to the topic?
- What should the learner do next?
- Write one sentence giving your opinion.

## Reading strategy
Read once for the main idea, read again for details, underline useful words, answer in your own words.
