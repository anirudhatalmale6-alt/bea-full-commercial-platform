## Parent/carer note
Practise English for 10-15 minutes. Ask the learner to explain stories, literature and creative expression using simple examples. Praise effort and confidence.

## Home tasks
- Read the word bank aloud.
- Ask and answer three questions.
- Write five sentences or record a short answer.
- Review one mistake and correct it kindly.
