Warm-up

Circle or write three words connected to Media, technology and digital citizenship: devices, apps, online behaviour, news, opinions, media.

Language practice

Use substitution and ellipsis. Complete: I can ___ because ___. My partner can ___ and ___.

Speaking task

Ask and answer three questions to interpret tone. Use polite turn-taking and one follow-up question.

Writing task

Write 5-8 sentences about Media, technology and digital citizenship. Include two words from the vocabulary bank and one correct example of substitution and ellipsis.
