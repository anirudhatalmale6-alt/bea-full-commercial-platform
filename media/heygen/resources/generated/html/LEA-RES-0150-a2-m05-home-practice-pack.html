Parent/carer note

Practise English for 10-15 minutes. Ask the learner to explain food, health and wellbeing using simple examples. Praise effort and confidence.

Home tasks

  • Read the word bank aloud.
  • Ask and answer three questions.
  • Write five sentences or record a short answer.
  • Review one mistake and correct it kindly.

Return evidence
