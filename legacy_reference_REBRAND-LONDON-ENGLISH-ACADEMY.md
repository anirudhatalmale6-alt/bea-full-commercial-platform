# British English Academy Rebrand

This package has been rebranded to **British English Academy**.

## Updated names

- Platform: British English Academy
- Short name: LEA
- Product: British English Academy ESL Platform
- Test: British English Academy CEFR Placement Test
- Certificate: British English Academy Diagnostic Certificate
- Report: British English Academy CEFR Score Report

## Rebrand scope

Updated homepage, placement-test journey, report/certificate labels, curriculum references, package identifiers, database names, documentation and deployment naming.

External benchmark names such as Cambridge, Pearson, McGraw Hill, CEFR, SplashLearn, Starfall and CoursesOnline may still appear where they refer to third-party benchmark sources. They should not be presented as ownership, endorsement or formal accreditation.

## Compliance note

British English Academy is an independent ESL learning and assessment platform. Its diagnostic certificate should not be described as an official Cambridge, Pearson, Oxford University, McGraw Hill, Council of Europe or government qualification unless formal recognition or accreditation is obtained.
