# British English Academy — Brand Brief

**British English Academy** is a premium digital English language platform providing CEFR-aligned placement testing, level-based ESL courses, interactive practice activities, score reports and verifiable diagnostic certificates.

## Positioning

British English Academy should feel academically credible, modern, international, practical and trustworthy.

## Core message

**Find your English level. Start your London pathway.**

## Product architecture

1. Paid CEFR placement test
2. Score report and diagnostic certificate
3. A1–C2 course pathway
4. Interactive ESL practice activities
5. Institution dashboard and group licensing

## Certificate wording

Use: **British English Academy Diagnostic Certificate**

Avoid wording that implies official Cambridge, Pearson, Oxford University, McGraw Hill, Council of Europe or government accreditation unless accreditation has been obtained.
