# British English Academy Worksheet
## B2-M02-L02 - Professional and Academic Skills: Vocabulary Lesson 2

**CEFR level:** B2  
**Skill:** vocabulary  
**Objective:** Learners can argue, evaluate, clarify and organise detailed communication in a vocabulary task about culture.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise vocabulary for professional and academic skills. You will learn useful language connected to culture and practise modal perfect. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use modal perfect to talk about culture.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using modal perfect.
2. Match the culture phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about culture. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B2 response about culture. Include the target language: modal perfect.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about culture using language from this lesson.
