# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M04-L05 - Problems, Advice and Decisions: Speaking Lesson 5

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** speaking  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a speaking task about education.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise speaking for problems, advice and decisions. You will learn useful language connected to education and practise conditionals 0/1/2. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use conditionals 0/1/2 to talk about education.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using conditionals 0/1/2.
2. Match the education phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about education. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about education. Include the target language: conditionals 0/1/2.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about education using language from this lesson.
