# British English Academy Worksheet
## A2-M02-L03 - Past Experiences and Personal Stories: Reading Lesson 3

**CEFR level:** A2  
**Skill:** reading  
**Objective:** Learners can describe, compare and exchange simple information in a reading task about work.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise reading for past experiences and personal stories. You will learn useful language connected to work and practise countable/uncountable nouns. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at A2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use countable/uncountable nouns to talk about work.
- Example 2: The learner gives a A2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for A2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using countable/uncountable nouns.
2. Match the work phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about work. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short A2 response about work. Include the target language: countable/uncountable nouns.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about work using language from this lesson.
