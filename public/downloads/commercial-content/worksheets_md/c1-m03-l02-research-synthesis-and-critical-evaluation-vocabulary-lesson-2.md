# British English Academy Worksheet
## C1-M03-L02 - Research, Synthesis and Critical Evaluation: Vocabulary Lesson 2

**CEFR level:** C1  
**Skill:** vocabulary  
**Objective:** Learners can synthesise, justify, negotiate and adapt register in a vocabulary task about critical thinking.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise vocabulary for research, synthesis and critical evaluation. You will learn useful language connected to critical thinking and practise advanced modality. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use advanced modality to talk about critical thinking.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using advanced modality.
2. Match the critical thinking phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about critical thinking. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C1 response about critical thinking. Include the target language: advanced modality.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about critical thinking using language from this lesson.
