# British English Academy Worksheet
## A2-M06-L02 - A2 Review, Fluency and Functional Tasks: Vocabulary Lesson 2

**CEFR level:** A2  
**Skill:** vocabulary  
**Objective:** Learners can describe, compare and exchange simple information in a vocabulary task about travel.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise vocabulary for a2 review, fluency and functional tasks. You will learn useful language connected to travel and practise past simple. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at A2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use past simple to talk about travel.
- Example 2: The learner gives a A2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for A2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using past simple.
2. Match the travel phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about travel. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short A2 response about travel. Include the target language: past simple.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about travel using language from this lesson.
