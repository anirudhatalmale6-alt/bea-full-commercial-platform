# British English Academy Worksheet
## B2-M04-L05 - Writing Arguments and Reports: Speaking Lesson 5

**CEFR level:** B2  
**Skill:** speaking  
**Objective:** Learners can argue, evaluate, clarify and organise detailed communication in a speaking task about business.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise speaking for writing arguments and reports. You will learn useful language connected to business and practise mixed conditionals. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use mixed conditionals to talk about business.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using mixed conditionals.
2. Match the business phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about business. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B2 response about business. Include the target language: mixed conditionals.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about business using language from this lesson.
