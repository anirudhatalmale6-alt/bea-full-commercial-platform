# British English Academy Worksheet
## A2-M04-L05 - Health, Weather and Plans: Speaking Lesson 5

**CEFR level:** A2  
**Skill:** speaking  
**Objective:** Learners can describe, compare and exchange simple information in a speaking task about shopping.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise speaking for health, weather and plans. You will learn useful language connected to shopping and practise present continuous. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at A2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use present continuous to talk about shopping.
- Example 2: The learner gives a A2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for A2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using present continuous.
2. Match the shopping phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about shopping. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short A2 response about shopping. Include the target language: present continuous.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about shopping using language from this lesson.
