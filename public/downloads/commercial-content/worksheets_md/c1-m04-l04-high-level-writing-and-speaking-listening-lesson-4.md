# British English Academy Worksheet
## C1-M04-L04 - High-Level Writing and Speaking: Listening Lesson 4

**CEFR level:** C1  
**Skill:** listening  
**Objective:** Learners can synthesise, justify, negotiate and adapt register in a listening task about professional communication.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise listening for high-level writing and speaking. You will learn useful language connected to professional communication and practise inversion. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use inversion to talk about professional communication.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using inversion.
2. Match the professional communication phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about professional communication. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C1 response about professional communication. Include the target language: inversion.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about professional communication using language from this lesson.
