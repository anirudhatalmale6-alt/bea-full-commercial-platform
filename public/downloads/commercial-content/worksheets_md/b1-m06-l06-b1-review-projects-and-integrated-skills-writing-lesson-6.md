# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M06-L06 - B1 Review, Projects and Integrated Skills: Writing Lesson 6

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** writing  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a writing task about relationships.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise writing for b1 review, projects and integrated skills. You will learn useful language connected to relationships and practise relative clauses. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use relative clauses to talk about relationships.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using relative clauses.
2. Match the relationships phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about relationships. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about relationships. Include the target language: relative clauses.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about relationships using language from this lesson.
