# British English Academy Worksheet
## B2-M06-L06 - B2 Review, Presentations and Integrated Assessment: Writing Lesson 6

**CEFR level:** B2  
**Skill:** writing  
**Objective:** Learners can argue, evaluate, clarify and organise detailed communication in a writing task about research.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise writing for b2 review, presentations and integrated assessment. You will learn useful language connected to research and practise cleft sentences. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use cleft sentences to talk about research.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using cleft sentences.
2. Match the research phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about research. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B2 response about research. Include the target language: cleft sentences.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about research using language from this lesson.
