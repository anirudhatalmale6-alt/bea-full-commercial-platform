# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M01-L03 - Independent Communication and Opinions: Reading Lesson 3

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** reading  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a reading task about media.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise reading for independent communication and opinions. You will learn useful language connected to media and practise passive voice basics. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use passive voice basics to talk about media.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using passive voice basics.
2. Match the media phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about media. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about media. Include the target language: passive voice basics.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about media using language from this lesson.
