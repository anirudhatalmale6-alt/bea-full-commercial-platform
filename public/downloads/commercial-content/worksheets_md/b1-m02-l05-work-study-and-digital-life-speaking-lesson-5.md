# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M02-L05 - Work, Study and Digital Life: Speaking Lesson 5

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** speaking  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a speaking task about environment.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise speaking for work, study and digital life. You will learn useful language connected to environment and practise modals of advice. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use modals of advice to talk about environment.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using modals of advice.
2. Match the environment phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about environment. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about environment. Include the target language: modals of advice.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about environment using language from this lesson.
