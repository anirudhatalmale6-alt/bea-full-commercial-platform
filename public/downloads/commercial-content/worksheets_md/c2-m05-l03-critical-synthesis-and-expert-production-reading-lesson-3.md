# British English Academy Worksheet
## C2-M05-L03 - Critical Synthesis and Expert Production: Reading Lesson 3

**CEFR level:** C2  
**Skill:** reading  
**Objective:** Learners can critique, refine, interpret nuance and produce expert-level responses in a reading task about specialised discourse.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise reading for critical synthesis and expert production. You will learn useful language connected to specialised discourse and practise stylistic inversion. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use stylistic inversion to talk about specialised discourse.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using stylistic inversion.
2. Match the specialised discourse phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about specialised discourse. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C2 response about specialised discourse. Include the target language: stylistic inversion.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about specialised discourse using language from this lesson.
