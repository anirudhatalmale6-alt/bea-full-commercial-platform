# British English Academy Worksheet
## C1-M01-L06 - Advanced Academic and Professional Discourse: Writing Lesson 6

**CEFR level:** C1  
**Skill:** writing  
**Objective:** Learners can synthesise, justify, negotiate and adapt register in a writing task about nuance.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise writing for advanced academic and professional discourse. You will learn useful language connected to nuance and practise complex discourse markers. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use complex discourse markers to talk about nuance.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using complex discourse markers.
2. Match the nuance phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about nuance. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C1 response about nuance. Include the target language: complex discourse markers.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about nuance using language from this lesson.
