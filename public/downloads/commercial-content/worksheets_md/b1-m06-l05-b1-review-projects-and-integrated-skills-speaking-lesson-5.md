# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M06-L05 - B1 Review, Projects and Integrated Skills: Speaking Lesson 5

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** speaking  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a speaking task about technology.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise speaking for b1 review, projects and integrated skills. You will learn useful language connected to technology and practise reported speech basics. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use reported speech basics to talk about technology.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using reported speech basics.
2. Match the technology phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about technology. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about technology. Include the target language: reported speech basics.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about technology using language from this lesson.
