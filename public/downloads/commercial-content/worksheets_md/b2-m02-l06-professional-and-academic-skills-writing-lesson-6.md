# British English Academy Worksheet
## B2-M02-L06 - Professional and Academic Skills: Writing Lesson 6

**CEFR level:** B2  
**Skill:** writing  
**Objective:** Learners can argue, evaluate, clarify and organise detailed communication in a writing task about academic topics.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise writing for professional and academic skills. You will learn useful language connected to academic topics and practise advanced passives. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use advanced passives to talk about academic topics.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using advanced passives.
2. Match the academic topics phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about academic topics. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B2 response about academic topics. Include the target language: advanced passives.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about academic topics using language from this lesson.
