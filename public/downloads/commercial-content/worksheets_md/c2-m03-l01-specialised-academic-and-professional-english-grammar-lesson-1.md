# British English Academy Worksheet
## C2-M03-L01 - Specialised Academic and Professional English: Grammar Lesson 1

**CEFR level:** C2  
**Skill:** grammar  
**Objective:** Learners can critique, refine, interpret nuance and produce expert-level responses in a grammar task about persuasion.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise grammar for specialised academic and professional english. You will learn useful language connected to persuasion and practise subtle modality. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use subtle modality to talk about persuasion.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using subtle modality.
2. Match the persuasion phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about persuasion. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C2 response about persuasion. Include the target language: subtle modality.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about persuasion using language from this lesson.
