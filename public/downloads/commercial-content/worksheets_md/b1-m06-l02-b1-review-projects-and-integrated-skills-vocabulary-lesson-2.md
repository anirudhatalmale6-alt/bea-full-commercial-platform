# British English Academy Worksheet
## B1-M06-L02 - B1 Review, Projects and Integrated Skills: Vocabulary Lesson 2

**CEFR level:** B1  
**Skill:** vocabulary  
**Objective:** Learners can explain, narrate, give reasons and manage familiar situations in a vocabulary task about workplace.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise vocabulary for b1 review, projects and integrated skills. You will learn useful language connected to workplace and practise present perfect. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use present perfect to talk about workplace.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using present perfect.
2. Match the workplace phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about workplace. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B1 response about workplace. Include the target language: present perfect.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about workplace using language from this lesson.
