# British English Academy Worksheet
## B2-M01-L06 - Complex Communication and Debate: Writing Lesson 6

**CEFR level:** B2  
**Skill:** writing  
**Objective:** Learners can argue, evaluate, clarify and organise detailed communication in a writing task about argument.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise writing for complex communication and debate. You will learn useful language connected to argument and practise advanced linking. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at B2 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use advanced linking to talk about argument.
- Example 2: The learner gives a B2 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for B2.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using advanced linking.
2. Match the argument phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about argument. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short B2 response about argument. Include the target language: advanced linking.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about argument using language from this lesson.
