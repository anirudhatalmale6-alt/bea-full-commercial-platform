# British English Academy Worksheet
## C1-M06-L03 - C1 Review, Portfolio and Performance Assessment: Reading Lesson 3

**CEFR level:** C1  
**Skill:** reading  
**Objective:** Learners can synthesise, justify, negotiate and adapt register in a reading task about research.

### 1. Learn
In this lesson you will practise reading for c1 review, portfolio and performance assessment. You will learn useful language connected to research and practise subjunctive forms. By the end, you should be able to complete a short real-life task and explain your answer clearly at C1 level.

### 2. Examples
- Example 1: I can use subjunctive forms to talk about research.
- Example 2: The learner gives a C1 answer with clear meaning and correct key language.
- Example 3: The learner checks, improves and repeats the answer after feedback.
- Example 4: The learner uses one linking phrase or support phrase suitable for C1.

### 3. Practice
1. Choose the best sentence using subjunctive forms.
2. Match the research phrase to its meaning.
3. Build a complete answer for the lesson task.

### 4. Speaking Task
Speak for 60-120 seconds about research. Use at least two examples and one reason.

### 5. Writing Task
Write a short C1 response about research. Include the target language: subjunctive forms.

### 6. Homework
Write or record five sentences about research using language from this lesson.
