### Distractors
- Wrong tense for countable/uncountable nouns
- Unclear word order
- Answer does not match context
- Too short for the task

### Feedback
- Correct: Correct. Continue to the next step.
- Partly correct: Good start. Check accuracy and add one more detail.
- Incorrect: Try again. Review the model and look at the key language.
