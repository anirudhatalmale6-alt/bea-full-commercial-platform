# A2 Everyday English Course Completion Pack

## Completion rule
Learner completes at least 80% of lessons, passes all module assessments, and passes the final level assessment with 70% or higher.

## Included evidence
- course progress report
- final level assessment result
- speaking/writing moderation record
- downloadable certificate
- next-level recommendation

## Certificate title
British English Academy A2 Course Completion Certificate

This is a British English Academy course completion certificate. It is not an official Council of Europe, Cambridge, Pearson, Oxford University or government qualification.
